French Toast

Description

This French Toast recipe is from user Jan Bittner and can be found on allrecipes. This recipe uniquely utilizes flour to coat the bread. A key thing to remember while make the French Toast is that the griddle/frying pan should be hot before placing the bread on it.

Prep time is 10 minutes. Cook time is 20 minutes. This recipe makes 12 servings (12 slices of toast)

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 3 eggs
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 12 thick slices bread

Steps

  1. Measure flour into a large mixing bowl. Slowly whisk in the milk. Whisk in the salt, eggs, cinnamon, vanilla extract and sugar until smooth.
  2. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium heat
  3. Soak bread slices in mixture until saturated. Cook bread on each side until golden brown. Serve hot.
